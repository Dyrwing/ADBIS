Our Mission

Region H Psykiatri exists to transform how people connect with psychiatric research opportunities, making participation more accessible, transparent, and rewarding for everyone.

We believe that progress in mental health research depends on bringing together diverse participants and innovative researchers in collaborative, ethical studies. By removing barriers to participation and providing a platform that respects the needs of both participants and researchers, we're helping to accelerate discoveries that can improve mental health care for all.

For Participants

We empower individuals to find and join research studies that match their interests and experiences, turning their participation into a meaningful contribution to mental health advancement.

For Researchers

We provide researchers with tools to connect with motivated, appropriate participants, helping them conduct higher-quality studies more efficiently and with greater demographic diversity.

Our Values

Ethics First

We uphold the highest ethical standards in research participation, ensuring that all studies on our platform respect participant autonomy, privacy, and wellbeing.

Transparency

We provide clear, accessible information about each research opportunity, empowering participants to make informed decisions about their involvement.

Inclusivity

We actively work to increase diversity in psychiatric research by making opportunities accessible to historically underrepresented populations.

Scientific Rigor

We support high-quality research by facilitating connections between researchers and appropriate participants, enhancing the validity and reliability of findings.

Our Commitment to Ethics

Every study on Region H Psykiatri undergoes a thorough review process to ensure it meets international ethical standards for human subjects research. We verify that studies have appropriate institutional approvals, informed consent procedures, and data protection measures in place.

We're proud to comply with GDPR requirements and the Declaration of Helsinki ethical principles. Our platform is designed to protect participant privacy while facilitating valuable research connections.
